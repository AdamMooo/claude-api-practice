# One-Day Meal Plan for Marathon Training Athlete

Here is a one-day meal plan for an athlete aiming to build endurance capacity with carbohydrate loading and optimal hydration:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 3,200 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (130g), Fat (85g), Carbs (480g)

## Meal Plan:

* **Breakfast (6:30 AM):** Oatmeal (100g dry weight) with banana (150g), honey (20g), and whole milk (240g). Side of whole wheat toast (50g) with almond butter (20g).
    * Protein: 18g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 95g

* **Pre-Training Snack (8:30 AM):** Energy bar (60g) with sports drink (500ml containing 30g carbs).
    * Protein: 8g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 60g

* **Lunch (12:30 PM):** Grilled chicken breast (150g) with brown rice (150g cooked), steamed sweet potato (150g), and mixed vegetables (carrots 75g, peas 75g). Olive oil drizzle (10ml).
    * Protein: 45g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 95g

* **Afternoon Snack (3:00 PM):** Greek yogurt (200g) with granola (40g) and mixed berries (100g). Electrolyte drink (500ml).
    * Protein: 22g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 65g

* **Dinner (6:30 PM):** Lean ground turkey (160g) pasta with whole wheat pasta (120g cooked), marinara sauce (150g), and side salad with olive oil dressing (30g dressing).
    * Protein: 35g, Fat: 18g, Carbs: 80g

* **Evening Snack (8:30 PM):** Rice cakes (40g) with honey (15g) and banana (100g).
    * Protein: 4g, Fat: 2g, Carbs: 85g

* **Hydration Throughout Day:** 3-4 liters of water + electrolyte sports drinks during training to support endurance performance and recovery.

This meal plan prioritizes complex carbohydrates for sustained energy, adequate protein for muscle recovery, and strategic timing around training sessions to maximize marathon preparation.
